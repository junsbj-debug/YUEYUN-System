# Talisman

## The Next Generation Personality Hardware Wallet

Today's smart watches record your health.

Tomorrow's Talisman records your digital personality.

---

Every day, we leave traces in the digital world:

- Interests and preferences
- Ways of thinking
- Communication habits
- Decision-making patterns
- Growth of personal values

Together, these fragments define:

**Who you are.**

---

Today, most platforms preserve raw data:

- Audio recordings
- Photos
- Videos
- Chat histories
- Activity logs

They preserve your traces,

but not necessarily your personality.

---

Talisman explores a new question:

> **Can personality be preserved without permanently storing private data?**

---

## How It Works

With the user's authorization,

the device continuously learns behavioral patterns and communication habits.

Through a personality purification process,

raw data is transformed into personality vectors,

and the original content is removed according to predefined rules.

---

What remains is not recordings, photos, or chat histories,

but a personal:

### Personality Digital Mirror

A structured representation of personality traits,

growth trajectories,

and long-term continuity.

---

## User Ownership

Personality data always belongs to the user:

- Locally encrypted storage
- User-controlled authorization
- Portable across devices
- Available for personality growth analysis
- Foundation for future personality services

---

## Core Principle

**Preserve personality, not private data.**
